About This Website

Our Mission

This website was created to share fun, friendly, and easy-to-understand information about the amphibians that live in Wisconsin. Frogs, toads, and salamanders are often overlooked, yet they play a vital role in our ecosystems. Here, you can explore facts, learn about different species, and discover how unique these animals really are.

Why Amphibians?

Amphibians are some of the most fascinating creatures on Earth. They breathe through their skin, survive harsh winters, and adapt to environments ranging from forests to ponds. By highlighting them, this website aims to spark curiosity and encourage people to appreciate the wildlife around them.

About the Creator

I am a simple lover of all things amphibians and despite being an art major, I desire to study, appreciate, and love all things that live on our mudball.
