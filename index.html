Amphibians are a vital part of our ecosystem, they are indicators of the environment and important predators for many annoying pests. The whole class of amphibians is one of the most ancient lineages stretching back to the dawn of time. when the first vertebrate animals crawled from the shallow seas to the earth. There are many different families within the class of amphibians. This website is all about the ones native to Wisconsin. Take a moment to look at these fascinating creatures within our very backyard!
